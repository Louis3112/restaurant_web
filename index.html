ABOUT US

DEPOT ACCORD is a small chinese depot located in Pujasera Hayam Wuruk, on Hayam Wuruk Street Number 1.

From savory dim sum and wok-fried specialties to comforting noodle soups and flavorful rice dishes, every bite is a Celebration of Taste and Tradition.

Established Since 2013, Depot Accord has served thousands of orders and still Counting to This Day. Despite relocating few times, Depot Accord still Committed to preserving the authentic taste and quality of the food we serve to our customers.
